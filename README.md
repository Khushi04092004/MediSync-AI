# MediSync AI

**AI-Powered Smart Pharmacy & Prescription Management System**

MediSync AI is an AI-assisted healthcare platform that simplifies prescription management, medicine ordering, inventory control, and future pharmacy automation. The platform combines AI-powered prescription analysis with secure verification, multilingual support, and healthcare management features to improve accessibility, efficiency, and patient safety.

---

## Overview

Traditional pharmacy systems rely heavily on manual prescription verification, inventory management, and medicine dispensing. This process is often slow, error-prone, and difficult to scale in hospitals and high-volume healthcare environments.

MediSync AI provides a modern digital solution that combines Artificial Intelligence, OCR, inventory management, secure authentication, and healthcare services into a single platform. The project is also designed to serve as the software foundation for a future AI-enabled medicine dispensing kiosk.

---

## Problem Statement

Healthcare institutions frequently face challenges such as:

- Manual prescription verification
- Difficulty reading handwritten prescriptions
- Long waiting times
- Inventory management issues
- Medicine dispensing errors
- Lack of centralized patient records

MediSync AI addresses these challenges using AI-assisted verification while ensuring that final medical decisions remain under professional supervision.
